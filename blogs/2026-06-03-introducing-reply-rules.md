Substack announces a new feature enabling creators to establish community guidelines through Reply Rules, with AI assistance that learns moderation patterns to help maintain the tone and standards each publication prefers. The tool gives writers more control over comment sections without requiring heavy manual moderation, letting the platform's AI adapt to each publication's unique community standards.
