Independent creator Jasmine Sun argues that writers possess significant competitive advantages in an AI-saturated landscape, emphasizing the irreplaceable value of original reporting, live interaction, and distinctive voice over algorithmic content. The piece makes the case that human authenticity and direct audience connection are assets that no AI-generated content can replicate, giving independent writers a structural edge on platforms like Substack.
